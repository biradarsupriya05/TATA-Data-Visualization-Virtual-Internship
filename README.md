# TATA-Data-Visualization-Virtual-Internship

### 📊 TATA Data Visualization Virtual Internship | Power BI

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard as part of the TATA Data Visualization Virtual Experience. The project focused on transforming business data into meaningful insights through data cleaning, modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visualizations. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, analyze revenue trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and support data-driven business decisions.

### 	🎯 Business Questions Solved

•	 Revenue Trend Analysis (CEO)
	The CEO wanted to view a time-series analysis of revenue for the year 2011 to understand monthly sales patterns and seasonal trends. A monthly revenue line chart was created to help forecast future sales performance.

•	 Top Countries by Revenue (CMO)
	The CMO wanted to identify the Top 10 countries generating the highest revenue along with quantity sold, excluding the United Kingdom to focus on international markets.
  
•	Top Customers by Revenue (CMO)
	A visualization was created to show the Top 10 customers contributing the highest revenue, sorted from highest to lowest. This helps the business focus on retaining valuable customers.
  
•	 Global Product Demand (CEO)
	A global demand map was created to identify countries with the highest product demand (excluding the UK). This insight helps leadership identify potential regions for business expansion
  
###	🛠 Tools & Technologies

•	Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization

•	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs

### 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

•	The following steps were performed before analysis:

•	Removed negative quantities representing product returns

•	Filtered Unit Price greater than 0

•	Removed missing Customer IDs

•	Excluded United Kingdom in specific visuals for expansion analysis
